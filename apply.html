How a Ruff Day became a company

Our Story

Founder Lani Tam, from San Francisco California, started Ruff Day to cope with the fact that she gets to see her doggo once every couple of months because she had moved to Seattle for college. The longest she has gone is 6 months. It's a horrible experience and she would not recommend it to anyone.

When Lani was sad all she wanted to do was to pet dogs. It was the only thing that made her happy somedays. She had found out that this need to boop some noses or pat some heads was not only hers, and thus Ruff Day began.

Lani started to contact all the people she knew who had doggos and paid them to borrow their doggo for an hour. The business skyrocketed and Ruff Day is now nationwide.
